If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it privately. Please do not disclose security issues publicly before they are reviewed and addressed.

## Supported Versions

Only the latest version on the `main` branch is currently supported.

## Reporting a Vulnerability (Public)
Email: **sthakur369@gmail.com**

Please include:
- Subject: [SECURITY REPORT] - "Short description of the issue.."
- One-sentence summary.
- Steps to reproduce (minimal commands or configuration).
- Expected vs actual behavior.
- Any PoC, sanitized logs, or minimal test case.
- Your preferred contact method and whether you want credit.

**Do not** include production user data or credentials in your initial report.

---

## Our Response
- Acknowledge within **3 business days**.
- Provide an initial assessment and expected next steps within **5 business days**.
- We will coordinate remediation and disclosure with you; we aim to fix high-severity issues as quickly as possible and will publish notices only after patches are available or by mutual agreement.

---

## Safe Harbor / Good Faith
We welcome responsible disclosure. We will not pursue legal action against individuals who act in good faith and follow this policy.

---

## Public Disclosure / Credit
We may publicly credit researchers (name, handle) unless you request anonymity. We may request a reasonable embargo period to develop and release a fix.

---

## Additional notes
- For security issues in published Docker images: include image tag, digest, and steps to reproduce.
- For issues affecting hosted/activation services (if any), specify the endpoint and timestamps.
